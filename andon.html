• Stars and their Types

WHAT
ARE STARS?

Stars are luminous celestial bodies made primarily of hydrogen and helium that produce light and heat through nuclear fusion in their cores.

TYPES OF STARS

Stars come in various types based on their size, age, and brightness. Some main types include Protostars, Main Sequence Stars, Red Giants, White Dwarfs, Neutron Stars, and Supergiants .
